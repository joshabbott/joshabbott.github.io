I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow in cognitive science at the University of Melbourne. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and cognitive science, where I'm interested in computational models of both artificial and human intelligence.

In particular, I explore ways in which people learn and reason with concepts and categories of things like colors and animals, across the world's cultures.
